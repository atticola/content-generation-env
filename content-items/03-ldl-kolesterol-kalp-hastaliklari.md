```
How Does LDL Cholesterol Cause Heart Disease?
```

### Content Long

LDL (Low-Density Lipoprotein) cholesterol is among the most important causes of cardiovascular disease. Known as "bad cholesterol," LDL accumulates in artery walls, leading to atherosclerosis development.

**The mechanism:**
1. LDL accumulation in artery walls
2. Oxidation and inflammation
3. Plaque formation
4. Narrowing and blockage
5. Acute event risk (heart attack)

Numerous studies show that lowering LDL cholesterol reduces heart attack and stroke risk. The lower the LDL, the lower the risk.

_Consult your doctor about your LDL targets._
